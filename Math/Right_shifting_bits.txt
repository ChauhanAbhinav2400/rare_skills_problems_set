========================================================================================
Right Shift
========================================================================================
Description
You learned that shifting left by n multiplies by 2ⁿ.

Right shifting is the opposite:

Shifting right by 1 divides by 2 (rounding down).
Shifting right by 2 divides by 2² = 4.
Shifting right by 3 divides by 2³ = 8.
So in general:
x >> n = floor(x ÷ 2ⁿ)

Example:
12 >> 1 = 6
12 >> 2 = 3
12 >> 3 = 1

Notice that the result always gets smaller, and odd numbers get rounded down because the last bit “falls off.”
